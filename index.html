Workday Scheduler

A simple calendar app for scheduling your work day

Welcome - To use this schedule:
  • Click on any of the time slots to add events to your schedule.
  • After adding an event make sure you press the corresponding 'Save Button' to the right of the text area.
  • The default display shows 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, but by clicking the 'Set Workday Hours' button you can choose any time-frame you want.
  • This schedule also has a Drag and Drop feature, so if you want to change the time of an event you already added, click and hold the 'Save Button', then drag the item to the new timeslot and the change will automatically save.
  • Have fun and be productive!
